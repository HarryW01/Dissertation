The Python documentation lists 69 total built-in functions. Of these, 18 listed below were deemed simple enough for the learner to implement. 4 are excluded from this list: \codeword{list}, \codeword{tuple}, \codeword{set}, and \codeword{dict}. These are restricted differently, only when their corresponding datatype is a restricted feature.
